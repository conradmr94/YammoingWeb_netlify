Understanding Health Scores

Every product gets a 1-100 score based on comprehensive analysis

70-100

Excellent/Good

Encourage

These products support your health. Rich in nutrients, minimal processing, safe ingredients.

31-69

Fair

Moderate

Consume mindfully. Some nutritional value but may have concerns like added sugars or processing.

1-30

Poor/Bad

Minimize

Limit consumption. High in unhealthy ingredients, heavily processed, or contains harmful additives.

9 Health Domains

Comprehensive analysis across multiple dimensions

Nutrient Density

Vitamins, minerals, and beneficial nutrients. Analysis of 30+ micronutrients with RDA comparisons.

Metabolic Load

Sugar, fat, fiber, and protein balance. Glycemic load calculation and macronutrient quality assessment.

Processing Level

NOVA classification (1-4 scale). Identifies ultra-processed foods and industrial ingredients.

Additives Safety

E-numbers and 600+ food additives analyzed. Risk ratings from safe to dangerous.

Microbiome Impact

Gut health effects from probiotics, prebiotics, and potentially harmful ingredients.

Personal Risk

Allergens, dietary restrictions, and health conditions. Instant warnings for your specific needs.

Environmental Impact

Sustainability and packaging assessment. Eco-Score integration.

Category Normalization

Fair comparison within food categories. Chocolate vs chocolate, not chocolate vs broccoli.

Cosmetic/Cleaning Safety

For non-food products. Toxicity, hormone disruptors, and carcinogen detection.
