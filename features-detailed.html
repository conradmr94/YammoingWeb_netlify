üè≠ Processing Level Scorer

+

NOVA Classification System:

Group 1: Unprocessed/Minimally Processed (Score: 90-100)

Examples: Fresh fruits, vegetables, grains, legumes, nuts, meat, milk

Processing: Cleaning, removal of inedible parts, drying, crushing, grinding, freezing, pasteurization

Key: No added substances

Group 2: Processed Culinary Ingredients (Score: 70-89)

Examples: Oils, butter, sugar, salt

Processing: Pressing, refining, grinding, milling

Key: Extracted from Group 1, used in cooking

Group 3: Processed Foods (Score: 40-69)

Examples: Canned vegetables, canned fish, cheeses, freshly made bread

Processing: Canning, bottling, fermentation

Key: Group 1 + Group 2 (2-3 ingredients), still recognizable

Group 4: Ultra-Processed Foods (Score: 1-39)

Examples: Soft drinks, packaged snacks, reconstituted meats, frozen meals

Processing: Industrial formulations (5+ ingredients)

Key: Contains hydrogenated oils, HFCS, protein isolates, emulsifiers, colors, flavors

Penalty: -20 to -40 points

Processing Indicators:

  • Ingredient count (more = higher processing)
  • Industrial ingredients (emulsifiers, stabilizers)
  • Reconstituted ingredients
  • Home-cooking replicability test
